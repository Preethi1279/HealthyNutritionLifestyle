Nutritional Advice

  1. Include a variety of fruits and vegetables in your meals to ensure a balanced nutrient intake.
  2. Stay hydrated throughout the day by drinking at least 8 glasses of water.
  3. Incorporate whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats into your daily diet.
  4. Avoid processed foods and opt for natural, minimally processed ingredients.
  5. Practice mindful eating by paying attention to portion sizes and hunger cues.
  6. Plan your meals in advance to avoid unhealthy last-minute food choices.
  7. Consider consulting a nutritionist to tailor your diet to specific health goals.

Ask Your Dietitians

Find answers to the most frequently asked questions about nutrition, recipes, and diet plans.

What is real food?

▼

Real food refers to whole, minimally processed foods that are close to their natural form, such as fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, whole grains, and healthy fats.

Are all of your recipes gluten-free?

▼

Not all recipes are gluten-free, but many of them are! Look for the gluten-free label on each recipe to ensure it meets your dietary needs.

Do your recipes contain nutrition information?

▼

Yes, we provide detailed nutrition information for most of our recipes, including calorie count and macronutrient breakdown.

Are your recipes geared toward weight loss?

▼

Some recipes are designed to support weight loss by being lower in calories and higher in nutrient density, but our main goal is overall health.

Do you offer nutrition consulting?

▼

Yes, we offer one-on-one nutrition consulting services to help you achieve your health goals.

Do you offer meal plans?

▼

Yes, we provide customizable meal plans based on your dietary needs and health objectives.

What is a dietitian?

▼

A dietitian is a licensed health professional specializing in nutrition and dietetics to promote better health.

Are your recipes just for people with food allergies?

▼

No, our recipes cater to a wide range of dietary preferences and are suitable for everyone, not just those with food allergies.

How can I submit a recipe suggestion?

▼

You can submit your recipe suggestions by contacting us through our website or email.

How can I submit a nutrition question?

Do you offer classes?

▼

Yes, we offer virtual and in-person classes on nutrition, healthy cooking, and meal planning.

What is your food/health philosophy?

▼

Our philosophy emphasizes balance, variety, and moderation to create a sustainable, healthy lifestyle.

What if I don’t eat gluten-free? Will I like your recipes?

Absolutely! While we offer gluten-free options, many recipes are adaptable or naturally gluten-free, making them enjoyable for everyone.
